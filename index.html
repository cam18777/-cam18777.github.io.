  2. largest bookstore in the world
    Powell's Bookstore in Oregon, Portland, holds the title of the world's largest bookstore, boasting an expansive retail floor space of 1.6 acres. Often referred to as the "City of Books," Powell's offers a vast collection of both new and used books and occupies an entire city block. The bookstore features ten thematic rooms, each with its unique color theme (Green, Blue, Orange, Purple, Red, Gold, Pearl, Coffee, Rose, and a Rare Book Room), providing a diverse and immersive reading experience. Additionally, Powell's Bookstore includes a cafe, making it a comprehensive destination for book enthusiasts.
  3. The film industry is dependent on books
    Books play a crucial role in supporting various industries beyond education and health, significantly influencing our entertainment landscape. Approximately 51% of movies and series are either based on or inspired by literature, highlighting the substantial impact of books on visual storytelling. Productions adapted from books tend to garner more attention and affection from audiences, contributing to their success. The absence of books would pose challenges for the film industry, leading to a decline in both the quantity and quality of content produced.
